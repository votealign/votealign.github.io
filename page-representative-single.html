District

State Rep. Peter MacGregor was first elected to the Michigan House in November 2010. He represents the 73rd District, which includes the city of East Grant Rapids and the townships of Cannon, Courtland, Nelson, Oakfield, Plainfield, Spencer and Grand Rapids Charter Township.

Education

MacGregor graduated from the Michigan State University School of Business in 1988.

Professional

MacGregor is a former small-business owner and also served as Cannon Township supervisor, as a trustee and as a Cannon Township planning commissioner.
